Favorite Spots in Duluth, MN

by: David McGinley

This map highlights three of my favorite spots around Duluth, Minnesota, a city perched on the western tip of Lake Superior, one of the largest freshwater lakes on Earth. Canal Park is Duluth's iconic waterfront district where you can watch massive ore boats pass just feet away under the Aerial Lift Bridge. Enger Tower is a five-story bluestone observation tower on the hillside offering sweeping panoramas of the harbor and Wisconsin's Bayfield Peninsula. Bentleyville Tour of Lights transforms Bayfront Festival Park each winter into the largest free walk-through lighting display in the nation, drawing hundreds of thousands of visitors.
